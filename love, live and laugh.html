Love, Live and Laugh




Information:

Cooking time: 20 mins

Ingredients:
  • 50 ml - vodka espresso
  • 1 shot of espresso coffee
  • Cappuccino foam (milk froth)
  • 10 ml triple sec/sugar syrup
  • Cinnamon powder
  • 3 coffee beans


Procedure:
  1. Shake together vodka, espresso coffee and triple sec (sugar syrup).
  2. Float cappuccino foam over it and sprinkle cinnamon powder over the foam.
  3. Drop three coffee beans atop.
