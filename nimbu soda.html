Nimbu Soda




Information:

Cooking time: 15 mins
Serving: 1 person

Ingredients:
  • Mint leaves
  • 3 tsp Sugar (powdered)
  • 3 tsp Sugar syrup
  • Lemon juice
  • Lemon zest
  • Soda water
  • Ice cubes


Procedure:
  1. Put some mint leaves in a glass.
  2. Add equal amount of powdered sugar followed by sugar syrup to the glass.
  3. Mince the ingredients together.
  4. Now pour some lemon juice to the glass and mix well.
  5. Add chopped lemon pieces to the mix and pour soda water over it.
  6. Add ice cubes and garnish with a lemon zest.
  7. Serve chilled.
