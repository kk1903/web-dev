Bisi Bele Bhaat




Information:

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Total Time: 40 minutes
Yield: 2 servings

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup toor dal
  • 1 1/2 chopped vegetables
  • 4 Tbsp chana dal
  • 2 Tbsp urad dal
  • 2 tsp jeera
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 3 red chillies
  • 1 tsp couscous
  • 2 Tbsp grated coconut
  • 1 sprig of curry leaves
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 Tbsp jaggery
  • 1 tamarind
  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste

    Procedure:

    1. Cook the rice and toor dal and keep it aside.
    2. Soak the tamarind in hot water and extract the tamarind water.
    3. Boil the vegetables.
    4. In a pan heat oil and add chana dal, urad dal, jeera, fenugreek, pepper, red chilli. Fry until dal turns light brown.
    5. Fry couscous and curry leaves. Grind the fried ingredients with coconut.
    6. Boil the tamarind water for 1 to 2 minutes. Add turmeric powder, salt and jaggery to it.
    7. Add vegetables and allow it to boil for 5 to 6 minutes.
    8. Add the grounded mixture. Let it boil for 2 to 3 minutes.
    9. Add toor dal followed by rice and cook for 2 minutes.
    10. Heat oil in a pan and fry groundnut, channa dal, urad dal and mustard.
    11. Add the fired ingredients to the bisi belebath.
    12. Garnish with boondhi and coriander leaves. Serve hot.
